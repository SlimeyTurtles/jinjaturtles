Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the DNHS Cooking Club?

The purpose of the DNHS Cooking CLub is to allow students to learn essential skills for college and to share their favorite dishes with peers who love food!

Can we receive emails?

Yes! By signing up you will receive our emails. Make sure to go to the sign up page!

Who is the creator of this club?

The creator of this club is Avinh and he has always had a great passion for not only eating food but cooking it as well!

When and where are meetings?

The meetings are Thursdays 3:45pm - 5pm in room A126.

Do I have to attend all of the meetings to be apart of the club?

No you do not have to attend every meeting, BUT please do your best to come to all the meetings you can!

What food will we be making?

All types of food! You come in with something you really want to make, let Avinh know and we can make it!
